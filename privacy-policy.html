Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Digital Drift respects your privacy. This page explains the information this website may collect, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Digital Drift does not require visitors to create an account or provide personal information to read articles. If you contact the site by email, the information you provide, such as your name, email address, and message, may be used only to respond to your inquiry or continue the related conversation.

Analytics and Cookies

This website uses Google Analytics to understand general visitor activity, such as pages viewed, approximate location, device type, referral source, and time spent on the site. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect this information.

Analytics data is used to improve the content, performance, and usability of Digital Drift. It is not used to identify you personally. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You may also use browser tools that limit analytics tracking.

Any information collected through this website may be used to operate and improve the site, understand aggregate audience behavior, respond to messages, protect the site from misuse, and comply with legal obligations when required.

Articles may link to third-party websites, documentation, services, GitHub repositories, or social platforms. Digital Drift does not control those websites and is not responsible for their content, availability, or privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

Data Sharing and Retention

Digital Drift does not sell personal information. Information submitted by email is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to the request, maintain correspondence, or meet applicable legal requirements. Analytics information is processed by Google according to its own policies.

Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect the website and communications. However, no internet transmission or storage system is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your Choices

You can choose not to send personal information by email, manage cookies in your browser, and request access to or deletion of personal information you previously sent through the contact channel, subject to applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated when the site, its services, or legal requirements change. The latest version will always be available on this page, together with its updated date.
